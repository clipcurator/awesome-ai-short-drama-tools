## Why AI Short Drama Is Not Just Text-to-Video

Many people evaluate AI short drama tools as if they were only text-to-video generators. That is usually too shallow.

Short drama creation often requires a multi-stage workflow:

1. turn an idea into a clear story concept
2. build the world, conflict, and rules
3. define characters, motivations, and relationships
4. generate episode outlines and scripts
5. create consistent characters and scenes
6. plan shots and camera movement
7. produce short-form video outputs

That is why end-to-end AI film studio platforms are becoming a separate category from simple video generators.

## A Practical Selection Framework

Before choosing an AI short drama tool, ask:

1. Do you start from an idea or an existing script?
2. Do you need only video output, or the entire story-to-video chain?
3. How important is character consistency across episodes?
4. Do you need scene continuity and storyboard control?
5. Do you need Chinese, English, or bilingual script generation?
6. Do you publish horizontal 16:9 content, vertical 9:16 content, or both?
